External and Enrollment Reporting Analyst

Institution:
Collin College
Location:
McKinney, TX
Category:
Admin - Institutional Research and Planning
Posted:
06/27/2020
Type:
Full-Time
External and Enrollment Reporting Analyst

Location:
3452 Spur 399,, Collin Higher Education Center
McKinney, Texas, 75069
United States

We are searching for an experienced External and Enrollment Reporting Analyst

Job Purpose:
Assist the Institutional Research Office (IRO) in the collection, processing, management and analysis of a variety of data sets
for internal and external institutional reporting.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Conduct research, analyze various types of data, and produce reports to support effective decision-making and policy formation within the district.
  • Collect, clean, and interpret data for internal consumption, and for federal, state and/or other external agencies/organizations reporting.
  • Extract, process, and submit data from/to various systems, including, but not limited to, the Collin College Student
  • Information System, the Collin College Data Warehouse, the Texas Higher Education Coordinating Board (THECB), and the
  • Integrated Post-secondary Education Data System (IPEDS).
  • Collaborate with the Manager of External and Enrollment Reporting to generate and validate state reports, federal reports, and other designated external reports as needed.
  • Collect and analyze data using software tools/packages as needed.
  • Produce and maintain documentation of data and finished products.
  • Stay abreast of changes in laws, rules, policies, and procedures. Maintain knowledge of current trends, events and emerging issues. Identify information, programs, and resources useful to IRO.
Supplemental Functions
  • Perform other duties as assigned.
  • Perform all duties and maintain all standards in accordance with college policies, procedures and Core Values.
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
  • Basic knowledge of statistics, and the ability to understand and analyze quantitative information
  • Knowledge of federal and/or state reports and reporting procedures
  • Knowledge of R Programming
  • Knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite
  • Strong written, oral and interpersonal skills
  • Organizational and time management skills
  • Experience using statistical software such as SPSS, Stata, and/or SAS
  • Experience working with SQL and extracting data from large databases
