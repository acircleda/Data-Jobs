Data Scientist

Institution:
Virginia Tech
Location:
Blacksburg, VA
Category:
Admin - Information Technology - Programmer/Analyst
Posted:
01/06/2020
Type:
Full-Time

Job Description

The Analytics and Institutional Effectiveness office at Virginia Tech is looking for creative and passionate data professional to help develop and operationalize the data and analytical solutions that help decision makers at all levels of the institution gain necessary insights to improve services, processes, student and faculty experience, institutional effectiveness, and financial, human and facilities resource management. We are looking for a professional who is

  • Passionate about data and numbers
  • Innately curious and fast learners
  • Self-motivated, team players with a drive to get things done.

Required Qualifications

  • BS in Statistics, Computer Science, Operations Research, Mathematics, Physics, Economics, Finance, or other quantitative disciplines.
  • Good understanding of statistical methods and machine learning algorithms
  • Hands on experience in collecting, integrating, processing, and analyzing data
  • Strong programming skills in Python, R, SQL
  • Knowledge of open source or commercial data visualization libraries/tools
  • Advanced spreadsheet skills; ability to analyze, summarize and present quantitative and qualitative information clearly.
  • Ability to think creatively, and solve problems
  • Ability to work in highly collaborative and dynamic work environment
  • Effective written and oral communication skills
  • Detail-oriented but with an ability to understand the university environment and contribute broadly to a variety of university goals and projects.

Preferred Qualifications

  • MS in Statistics, Computer Science, Operations Research, Mathematics, Physics, Economics, Finance, or other quantitative disciplines.
  • Experience in applying a broad range of quantitative techniques (statistical modeling, machine learning...) to solve real world problems
  • Experience in data collection, integration, processing and analysis
  • Proficiency in Python, R
  • Experience in working with relational databases and proficiency in SQL scripting
  • Experience with open source or commercial data visualization tools/libraries such as Highcharts.js, Plot.ly, Tableau etc.
  • Experience in cloud computing with exposure to big data technologies (AWS, Apache Spark, Hadoop, Hive)

Pay Band

N/A

Salary Information

Commensurate with Experience

Review Date

January 21, 2020

Additional Information

The successful Candidate will be required to have a criminal conviction check

About Virginia Tech

Virginia Tech is a public land-grant university, committed to teaching and learning, research, and outreach to the Commonwealth of Virginia, the nation, and the world. Building on its motto of Ut Prosim (that I may serve), Virginia Tech is dedicated to InclusiveVT-serving in the spirit of community, diversity, and excellence. We seek candidates who adopt and practice the Principles of Community, which are fundamental to our on-going efforts to increase access and inclusion, and to create a community that nurtures learning and growth for all of its members. Virginia Tech actively seeks a broad spectrum of candidates to join our community in preparing leaders for the world.

Virginia Tech does not discriminate against employees, students, or applicants on the basis of age, color, disability, sex (including pregnancy), gender, gender identity, gender expression, genetic information, national origin, political affiliation, race, religion, sexual orientation, or veteran status, or otherwise discriminate against employees or applicants who inquire about, discuss, or disclose their compensation or the compensation of other employees or applicants, or on any other basis protected by law.
