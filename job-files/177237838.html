Data Analyst

Institution:
Washington State University
Location:
Pullman, WA
Category:
Admin - Institutional Research and Planning
Posted:
03/09/2020
Application Due:
03/15/2020
Type:
Full-Time
Data Analyst


Employee Type: CS-Classified Staff

Position Number: 87343

Work Location: Pullman

Summary of Duties:
The Business Data Analyst position will conduct high-level research activities and projects involving the compilation of complex data, mathematical and statistical analysis, and reporting of results. Develop and coordinate policies and procedures requiring specialized knowledge and skills in data analysis, data mining, and data visualization in the university context, including potentially sensitive data. Access and analyze data for assessment of student learning using OBIEE and other resources. Use university system to extract and format employee and financial information. Meet the Chancellor and campus administration's needs for assembly, analysis, and reports of complex data on the campus and its academic programs for evaluation of metrics, policy development, and strategic planning. Skills in modeling data to predict outcomes. Plan and manage collection, analyses, and reporting of assessment data in a highly distributed environment. Exercise independent judgment and discretion.

Required Qualifications:
A Bachelors degree including college level course in Statistics and/or Mathematics AND Three years of year experience working as a Data Consultant 3 or equivalent; OR equivalent education/experience.

Graduate training in related discipline may be substituted for up to two years of experience.

For those positions required to supervise, experience must include at least one year of supervisory experience.

Posting Open Date: 03/06/2020

Posting Close Date: 3/15/2020

Open Until Filled: No
