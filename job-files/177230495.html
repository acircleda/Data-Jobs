Institutional Research (IR) Analyst

Institution:
University of Rio Grande
Location:
Rio Grande, OH
Category:
Admin - Institutional Research and Planning
Posted:
02/28/2020
Application Due:
Open Until Filled
Type:
Full-Time
The University of Rio Grande/Rio Grande Community College is seeking a full-time Institutional Research (IR) Analyst to support the institution’s commitment to data informed decision-making. This full-time position is responsible for data analysis, data visualization, and data organization for decision support across the institution.

Position Description:
Job duties include (but are not limited to):· Timely and accurate submission of institutional data to regulatory agencies including the National Center for Educational Statistics (IPEDS reporting), Ohio Department of Higher Education (HEI and CCP reporting), the Higher Learning Commission annual institutional updates, and other external reporting;Gathering, validating, cleaning, and maintaining data to assist academic and support services; Designing, developing, implementing, and supporting data dashboards and reports; Collaborating with internal constituencies to identify and define research project requirements and parameters; and Identifying, analyzing, and interpreting trends or patterns in data sets including forecasting enrollment trends.

Minimum Qualifications:·
Bachelor’s degree in computer science, statistics, a quantitative social science, or related field. Working knowledge of relational databases and SQL. Advanced computer skills with expertise using Excel for manipulation, organization, analysis, and visualization of complex datasets, including a high level of familiarity with Power Point and Pivot Tables. Ability to retrieve, analyze, and present data in meaningful formats (tables, charts, graphs, etc.) Strong oral, written, and interpersonal skills with the ability to convey technical issues to a non-technical audience. Critical thinking ability to identify the underlying principles, reasons, or facts of an issue by breaking down data into separate parts. Capable of adapting, as needed, to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines.

Preferred Qualifications·
Master’s degree in computer science, statistics, or related field.· Experience in higher education.· Familiarity with HEI reporting protocols.· Experience performing qualitative analysis and/or sentiment analysis· Experience using R or Python for data analysis and visualization.

Application Instructions:
Qualified applicants must send a cover letter (detailing their interest and qualifications), current resume, as well as the names and contact information of at least three (3) references to: Chris Nourse, Director of Human Resources.

Review of applications will begin immediately. Position is open until filled. Background check and official transcripts required prior to hire. For additional information about Rio, please visit: www.rio.edu

The University of Rio Grande/Rio Grande Community College is America's unique private/public institution of higher education designed to provide learners the opportunity to attain a high-quality, high-value education. Our personalized, learner-centered environment promotes successful lives, careers, and responsible citizenship. The campus of the university and community college is nestled in the rolling hills of southeastern Ohio, and is adjacent to the original Bob Evans Farms and Homestead in Rio Grande. Rio is an Equal Opportunity Employer
