Assistant/Associate/Full Professor in Educational Measurement and Assessment

Institution:
Emirates College for Advanced Education
Location:
Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates
Category:
Faculty - Education - Edu. Admin and Leadership (study of)
Posted:
11/22/2020 (Reposted Job: Initially posted on 03/29/2020)
Type:
Full-Time
Required Qualifications:
  • An earned doctorate in educational measurement, educational statistics or a related field (e.g. quantitative psychology).
  • Evidence of methodological expertise in advanced educational measurement.
  • Demonstrated general knowledge and interest in teaching educational measurement and assessment.
  • Demonstrated investment in and commitment to mentoring and advising master’s and doctoral students.
  • Demonstrated record of research productivity commensurate with the position level.
  • Demonstrated ability to develop working research collaborations across disciplinary boundaries.
  • Evidence of generating external funding in support of research activities, as appropriate for faculty rank.
  • Evidence of engagement in professional organizations associated with the field (e.g. NCME, APA, AERA).
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Expertise in student assessment and testing in the K-12 environment.
  • Demonstrated ability in developing successful school and community partnerships.
  • Experiences working in linguistically and culturally diverse settings.
Strong candidates will have an active research program in an area such as: longitudinal or multilevel structural equation modeling (SEM), mixture models, latent space models, and/or SEM Bayesian analysis methods. Additionally, we anticipate that candidates will be familiar with ideas embodied in critical quantitative methodology, as well as possess applied analytic experience in one or more education content areas.

Interested candidates should submit a full CV and cover letter.

About Emirates College for Advanced Education (ECAE):

The Emirates College for Advanced Education (ECAE) is a higher education institution committed to research and teaching excellence, innovation, advancement and transformation in the field of education. The College is committed to playing a key role in the development of educational policy and practice in the UAE, while providing educators with world-class education and professional development opportunities. ECAE offers specialized and accredited post-graduate academic programs including Post Graduate Diplomas, Master of Education (M.Ed.) and Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Education programs. Furthermore, the College delivers national and international qualifications and training programs through its Continuing Education Centre (CEC).

ECAE has a reputation for excellence in education with highly quali?ed and experienced international faculty and trainers delivering its programs to learners who want to enhance their professional practice within the educational ?eld.

ECAE is a research-active and engaged College of Education and holds regular research conferences and seminars, supports colleagues through research groupings, and develops local, national and international partnerships with schools, universities and other educational agencies. We are committed to innovation and best practice with a focus on teaching and learning in a bilingual culture and to using our research to inform practice and policy development in the education system of the Emirate of Abu Dhabi, the UAE and beyond.

The Benefits:
ECAE's benefits package is highly attractive with competitive salaries free of tax in the United Arab Emirates, a generous cost of living allowance, annual vacation tickets for the employee and immediate family, educational subsidies for children and subsidized healthcare provided to the employee and sponsored family members.
