Data Analyst

Institution:
Lehigh University
Location:
Bethlehem, PA
Category:
Admin - Development and Fundraising
Posted:
01/21/2020
Type:
Full-Time
Position Number: S85390-20190911-2

Website:

https://gocampaign.lehigh.edu/

Summary:

The Data Analyst is responsible for providing analysis of institutional data and prospect management services to Development and Alumni Relations staff members who are responsible for engaging Lehigh's alumni. Prospect management is the tracking, auditing, and reporting of Lehigh's fundraising activity with alumni, parents and friends. The Data Analyst supports Lehigh's fundraising activities by serving in a collaborative and consultative partnership role with fundraisers and other teams. This position will work on processes associated with on-going relationship management, including building and maintaining fundraiser portfolios, defining and implementing methods to evaluate and drive portfolio productivity, and monitoring key measures of fundraiser activity. Additionally, this position monitors the effective and timely capture of relationship management data. This is a comprehensive position which includes tasks related to data entry, data analysis, and project management.

Accountabilities:
  1. Prospect management support
    • Execute solicitation updates for the major gifts team and other fundraisers, while adhering to prospect management departmental policies and procedures
    • Coordinate and respond to requests, prepare ad-hoc reports for staff, and provide instruction on prospect management areas of the database while maintaining data integrity and accuracy at all times
    • Assist in the gift credit process and manage the assignment and reassignment of donors
    • Works closely with the director and associate director to study internal and external data to help discover, analyze and maintain donor records for those who are capable of making philanthropic contributions to the University
    • Provide strategic analysis to ensure Lehigh's fundraising efforts are focused on working with the right donors at the right time
    • Manage Prospect Management anomaly reports and data clean-up efforts
    • Provide ongoing support to the fundraisers: respond to Prospect Management needs, questions and requests through in-person meetings, telephone and email
  2. Major Gift Portfolios and portfolio reviews
    • In collaboration with the Associate Director, manage and create portfolios of donors who can give philanthropically to Lehigh, routinely evaluating content, size, and value to align with fundraiser metrics and goals
    • Assist/lead in-person portfolio review meetings to help colleagues skillfully manage the university's donors and lead focused conversations about donors and portfolios
    • Assist in the development of strategies for engagement with donors through consultative portfolio reviews and provide recommendations
    • Meet with fundraisers to review portfolios of donors when there are staff transitions
  3. Data analysis and visualization
    • Demonstrate the ability to understand, create and manipulate data sets using Excel, Tableau, and/or other statistical software
    • Utilize appropriate tools for the visualization of data and its analysis; organize visualizations into useful delivery systems
    • Participate in the implementation and integration of a new CRM and the development of related prospect management business flow and processes
  4. Data management and project support
    • In conjunction with the Associate Director, evaluate priority levels for projects depending on need, impact and buy-in; set reasonable timelines and deadlines for project delivery
    • When communicating with colleagues, work to understand their point of view, define actionable and tangible objectives and determine the scope of the project
    • Record, collect and segment project data into a clear and concise manner allowing for transparency across Development and Alumni Relations
    • Assist on special projects associated with Lehigh's campaign and perform other duties as assigned
Qualifications:
  1. Bachelor's Degree in a field of study which emphasizes data analysis
  2. One to three years related work experience (Higher Education experience preferred)
  3. Strong oral and written communication and interpersonal skills
  4. Strong Microsoft Excel skills including pivot tables, basic functions, formatting, and visualizations
  5. Strong data visualizations skills (Tableau experience preferred)
  6. Ability to work independently on projects and as a part of a cohesive team
  7. Ability to analyze and synthesize data
  8. Ability to interact well with all levels of the organization for the purpose of acquiring information and prioritizing work assignment
  9. Ability to meet deadlines and work under time constraints
  10. Strong commitment to accuracy, attention to detail, and excellent judgement
  11. Solid computer skills with experience using word processing and spreadsheet software
  12. Successful completion of standard background checks including but not limited to: social security verification, education verification, county and national criminal background checks, PATCH, FBI fingerprinting, Child Abuse Clearance. Based upon the requirements of the position Lehigh may also request credit history reports, motor vehicle checks and professional license/certification verification
