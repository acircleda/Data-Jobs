Data Quality Analyst - Institutional Research

Institution:
CampusWorks, Inc.
Location:
Greeley, CO
Category:
Admin - Institutional Research and Planning
Posted:
12/09/2019
Type:
Full-Time
CampusWorks, Inc. has an amazing opportunity for a Data Analyst to support our client in the Greeley, Colorado area. THIS IS NOT A REMOTE ROLE. THE POSITION IS LOCATED IN GREELEY, CO.

Responsibilities
  • Analyze and interpret Banner data points and develop insight to inform the institution surrounding the quality of data
  • Develop and maintain data quality documentation and recommend modification per institutional requirements
  • Acquire, review, and monitor data from primary or secondary data sources to develop and maintain the Prism data lake
  • Review Referential integrity
  • Review historical integrity
  • Maintain a record of original data and corrected data
  • Maintain and develop the data dictionary in Workday to ensure adherence to data quality standards
Requirements
  • 2-5 year’s data analysis experience
  • Higher Education experience preferred
  • Ellucian Banner and ODS experience preferred
  • Programming languages such as Oracle PL/SQL required

About CampusWorks
Founded in 1999, CampusWorks is dedicated to helping higher education overcome business and technological challenges that stand in the way of student success and completion. We work with community colleges, technical colleges, four-year public and private institutions, multi-college districts, and statewide higher education systems to tackle problems big and small. Our services are rooted in systems thinking and reach across the institution—from Student Services to Human Resources to Finance to Academics—to achieve transformative results.
