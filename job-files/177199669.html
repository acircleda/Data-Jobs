Faculty Affairs Data Analyst

Institution:
California State University, Long Beach
Location:
Long Beach, CA
Category:
Admin - Institutional Research and Planning
Posted:
01/27/2020
Type:
Full-Time
Job ID: 1014227

Location:
Faculty Affairs

Full/Part Time: Full-Time

Regular/Temporary: Regular

Job Code 1038

Job Grade Exempt I

Department Faculty Affairs-0118

Salary
The salary range for this classification is $3,897 to $7,051 per month.

Department
Faculty Affairs

Time Base/Hours/Units per Week
Full-time, 40 hours per week.

Position Description
This position is responsible for providing comprehensive data support to the Associate Vice President for Faculty Affairs and various areas within Faculty Affairs via data compilation, research, analyses, and extensive reporting. Scope of support includes but is not limited to Faculty Affairs initiatives including Beach 2030, faculty personnel compliance, compensation, recruitment, performance evaluation, grievance investigations, and public records requests. The incumbent will also establish custom faculty databases and maintain data sets for trend analyses as well as ad hoc inquiries and analyses.

Knowledge, Skills & Abilities
Working knowledge of data management software, ie., Excel, Qualtrics, PeopleSoft, CS Link. Excellent writing and communication skills. Statistical analysis using appropriate software, ie, SPSS in order to facilitate data analysis. Knowledge of Tableau to visualize data. Ability to present data in creative and innovative ways appropriate to needs of specific audience.

Ability to communicate with an ethnically and culturally diverse campus community.  Ability to follow all university policies, procedures, and guidelines including but not limited to safety, civility, information security, and non-discrimination policies and procedures.  Ability to contribute to a positive university experience for each and every student, and assist in achieving the university's commitment to a "vision of excellence."

Education and Experience
Equivalent to a bachelor's degree in a related field and three years of related experience required. Four years of administrative work experience involving study, analysis, and/or evaluation leading to the development or improvement of administrative policies, procedures, practices, or programs is preferred.

Application Deadline
February 7, 2020.
