Organizational Overview

At VCU, we Make it Real through learning, research, creativity, service and discovery - the hallmarks of the VCU experience. A premier, urban, public research university nationally recognized as one of the best employers for diversity, VCU is a great place to work. It's a place of opportunity, where your success is supported and your career can thrive. VCU offers employees a generous leave package, career paths for advancement, competitive pay, and an opportunity to do mission-driven work.

Description of the Job

The School of Education is excited to invite candidates who will serve as a Research Associate with the Metropolitan Education Research Consortium (MERC). This position will work with the research/evaluation team within VCUs School of Education to lead and engage in research activities for an equity review for a state agency until September 2021. Duties include data collection and management, data analysis, and reporting.

Required Qualifications
  • Experience preparing and managing data for use with statistical programs (i.e, SPSS, Stata, R), including reviewing and cleaning incoming files, merging data sets, creating variables.
  • Experience running advanced regression analysis using a statistical program (i.e., SPSS, Stata, R).
  • Experience with a variety of data collection and analysis techniques using quantitative (i.e., surveys, secondary data) and qualitative data (i.e., interviews/focus groups, case studies, policy/document analysis).
  • Demonstrated experience working in fostering a diverse faculty, staff, and student environment, or commitment to do so as an employee at VCU.
  • Experience working on teams.
  • Strong interpersonal skills.
  • Strong writing and communication skills.
  • Masters degree in education, research, statistics, or a related field.
Preferred Qualifications
  • Doctoral degree in education, research, statistics, or a related field.
  • Experience conducting research and/or evaluation projects across various sectors, including government agencies, non-profits, PK12 Schools, and institutions of higher education.
  • Experience leading a research team.
  • Knowledge of health and human resources systems.
