Job Summary

UK Healthcare (UKHC) Performance Analytics Center of Excellence (PACE) has posted the Data Scientist - Principal role. This Role is a critical role in the transformational change at UK Healthcare to drive analytics as core infrastructure in support of the UKHC strategy: data as a strategic asset in a data-driven organization.

The Data Scientist - Principal is a key resource in the data analytics, data science, and data warehousing functions at UKHC. This role serves as the subject matter expert in advanced analytics and data science, including predictive and prescriptive analytics, artificial intelligence, machine learning, data mining, natural language processing, and data engineering. The role will keep abreast of the rapidly changing data science domain best practices, tools, and applications available, healthcare industry data requirements and drives the maturation of UK Healthcare in the use of data science best practices.

The Data Scientist - Principal works closely with data governance committees and workgroups and leadership to ensure best practices are followed. This role is responsible for maintenance and enhancement of advanced analytics solutions and to mentor, educate and drive best practices with analytics team members, data and business analysts, and researchers.

UK HealthCare is a comprehensive healthcare system consisting of the medical, nursing, health sciences, public health, dental and pharmacy patient care activities of the University of Kentucky in Lexington, KY, and in several off-site locations. UK HealthCare is expanding through the construction of a new hospital and potential future acquisitions. All UK HealthCare employees commit to providing excellent service and promoting a positive image while adhering to UK HealthCare Behavioral Expectations and Standards which include being: customer centered, service focused, quality driven, resource wise, and growth oriented.

Skills / Knowledge / Abilities

Qualified candidates must have demonstrated and extensive experience in decision support, data science methodologies such as statistical analysis and/or data modeling and data exploration and visualization. This individual must have strong knowledge of project management methodologies and practices as well as strong leadership, interpersonal, and financial management skills. The ideal candidate will have expert knowledge of Analytics, Business Intelligence and Data Science tools such as Tableau, Qlik, Cognos, Microsoft PowerBI, Python, R etc. as well as expert understanding of analysis of structured and unstructured data and of relational databases such as Microsoft SQL and a strong knowledge of enterprise data warehouse best practices. Must be a customer focused, determined leader who possesses strong facilitation, presentation, conflict management, problem solving and communications skills

Does this position have supervisory responsibilities?: No

Preferred Education/Experience

PhD Preferred IT, Data Science, Math, Finance, HealthCare, Statistics or related field.

Deadline to Apply: 02/02/2020
