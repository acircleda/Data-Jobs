Assessment Specialist

Institution:
Oklahoma State University
Location:
Stillwater, OK
Category:
Admin - Assessment, Accreditation, and Compliance
Posted:
02/19/2020
Type:
Full-Time
Req ID: req7909

Position Number: AS8753

Department: University Assessment and Testing

Hiring Supervisor: Kelva Hunger

Hiring Range
(Contingent upon available funding):
15.29 - 22.96, Hourly

Work Schedule: Monday - Friday, 8:00am - 5:00pm

Job Summary

This position involves preparing high-quality, professional reports of results for assessment and survey projects that will be presented to faculty members, administrators, staff, the Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education, and potentially as part of research reports for regional and national conferences. This responsibility requires excellent organization skills, attention to detail, knowledge of research design, qualitative analysis skills, the ability to communicate effectively, technical writing, and report preparation skills. Additional duties include facilitation of the new university-wide assessment management system, Nuventive Improve, oversee the Student Survey of Instruction, manage associated email inboxes, and aiding in program outcomes assessment review. Other duties as assigned.

Normal work hours are 8:00am - 5:00pm Monday-Friday. Additional hours outside of these times may be required during peak work periods.

Special Instructions to Applicants
Please include a resume, cover letter, and a list of references.

Position Qualifications

Minimum Qualifications:

Bachelor's Degree

One year of related experience.

Must be able to follow instructions exactly, maintain confidentiality, and uphold ethical standards. Rigorous attention to detail is required. Must possess excellent interpersonal, communication (written and verbal), and organizational skills. Must be able to provide excellent customer service, work independently, coordinate projects through to completion. Must be proficient working with technology and software, including databases, and maintaining confidential information appropriately and securely. Personal computer and/or word processing skills are also required.

Preferred Qualifications:

Master's Degree in Qualitative, Educational Research, Measurement, Statistics, or a related field

Three years of experience in a related field as well as with Nuventive Improve software.
